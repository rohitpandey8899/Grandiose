Customer Contacts Us

You may visit our office and discuss your requirements with our sales manager; he or she can show you products and designs that are more suitable for your requirements. Contact us through our website, by phone or by email if you work in another city. Dedicated customer service representatives will respond to your enquiry quickly and assign it to an experienced sales representative. Your requirements will be understood and the appropriate solution provided.

Requirement and Rough Estimate

An effective design project begins with an understanding of the client's needs. Communicating with your designer is therefore essential. We at 'The Grandiose' offer our clients a sense of control. Our interior designers have vast experience working on residential projects and providing completely bespoke design services. With a big focus on people, we can reinvent your homespace, whether you have an apartment or a villa, our designers have the vision to reimagine your homespace and the expertise to deliver a flawless interior fit-out. By understanding your requirements, we provide a rough estimate on the cost of the project. This depends on the certain aesthetic you’re trying to create, how you want each space to feel and budget.

Token payment and Actual Measurement of Space

Our team of experienced, qualified and professional designers make the trip to the client's site and take actual measurements after the initial estimate is provided. Everything is done at the client's convenience.

Personal Attention for Design

A 3D model of any room or space can be created based on the measurements and design specifications provided by the client. In the most basic form of this service, we offer our clients 3D models which are realistic and as close as possible to how the finished project will look like. We are able to give our clients a visual representation not only of how the color or furniture evolves in their home, but also how their living space becomes a whole. We pursue high-end design aesthetics to ensure that the properties we build provide enough value for our clients, and our designers have worked on some phenomenal residential projects all over the country.

Installation and Handover

Customer service is our company's core value. It takes more than meets the eye to design a home. There are so many valuable considerations to be made, from choosing colors to perfection in furniture. For the production and assembly of modular kitchens and furniture, we have factories with the most modern production lines. We offer our products at the best quality, always reliable and of the perfect aesthetics. Furthermore, our team ensures timely handover of your dream home.

After-Sales Services

It’s our goal to design spaces that completely transform the way people feel in their homes. Our after sales client relations and service team provides superior support following the completion of your project with us. Any interior questions you have can be answered by our experienced team, who can also provide advice regarding the use of materials and offer more inspiring suggestions. We offer 10 years warranty on our products and a life long support for our beloved clients. Feel free to contact us if you have any questions or concerns.In fact, we love to hear from our clients how they impressed their guests with our beautiful furniture.
